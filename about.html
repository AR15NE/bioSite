About Zoey Rovey

Zoey Lynn Rovey is a 5-year-old, spunky kindergartener living in rural Nebraska. She was born in Phoenix, Arizona, in 2019. She moved with her family in 2020 to northeast Nebraska so her dad could work for a local farm. She lives with her mom and dad, Jorgen and Amanda Rovey. She has two older brothers - Zaid (11) and Zacchaeus (7) - and a younger sister Zaylee (4).

Zoey has many hobbies which you can learn more about on the hobby page. Her favorite activities are arts and crafts, sports, and spending time with family. She is very close to her younger sister Zaylee. Together, they love to play dress up and create masterpieces to hang on the fridge. She also loves spending time with her extended family who live in Minnesota, including her grandma, grandpa, and aunties. In her local community, she is very involved in her church's Sunday school and VBS programs.

Zoey and her dad have a special bond. They both have been diagnosed and live with ADHD . Together, they work on embracing their strengths and learning about their limitations. Now in kindergarten, Zoey has been learning how to go with the flow even if it's not what she wants or expects. She has also been getting better at slowing down and trying her best. Zoey and her dad love to play sports together, board games, and video games. If you want to learn more about ADHD, you can visit the Child Mind Institute Website.

Described as spunky, energetic, and creative, Zoey radiates positivity and liveliness. Her outgoing, bubbly personality is inspiring, and she uses it to connect with friends and make others feel welcomed. Even at her young age, Zoey has many plans for the future. She hopes to become a veterinarian and a mom. She aspires to grow closer to her friends and family and develop her artistic skills. All in all, Zoey is contagiously optimistic and outgoing. Cherished dearly by her family, she serves as an inspiration to others, reminding everyone to be joyful and active.

The world is a much brighter place with Zoey Rovey in it.
